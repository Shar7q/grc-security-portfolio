# Risk Register — HandmadeHub (Case Study)

## Methodology
Risks scored using Probability × Impact matrix (1–5 scale).  
Risk Score = Likelihood × Impact. Scores ≥ 12 = High, 6–11 = Medium, ≤ 5 = Low.
